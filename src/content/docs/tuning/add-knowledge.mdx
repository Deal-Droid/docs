## How Your AI Uses Documents

Your Droid is a smart reader! When you upload a document, the system automatically breaks the information down into smaller, logical chunks. When a customer asks a question, your Droid finds the most relevant chunk of knowledge and uses it to give a precise and helpful answer.

You can use simple Markdown to structure your documents, which helps the AI understand the hierarchy and context of the information. Here are the basic symbols you can use:

```markdown
# A hashtag creates a main heading. More hashtags (##, ###) create subheadings.

- A hyphen creates a bullet point for an unordered list.

1. A number followed by a period creates an ordered, numbered list.

--- Three hyphens create a line to separate different sections of information.
```

## Document Fields Explained

![Document edit page](@/assets/general/document_edit.png)

Here's a field-by-field guide to adding a new document. Each field plays an important role in helping your AI understand and use your knowledge effectively.

### Title

This is the name of your document. It will be included with the content to give the AI context.

**Tips:**

- Use clear, descriptive titles
- Make it easy to identify the document's purpose
- Use natural language that makes sense to your team

**Examples:**

- "Return & Exchange Policy"
- "Shipping Information"
- "Product Care Guide"

---

### Content

This is the main body of your document, written in Markdown format.

**What to include:**

- Complete information about the topic
- Clear structure with headings and subheadings
- Bullet points or numbered lists for easy reading
- All relevant details your AI needs to answer customer questions

### Adding Images to Your Documents

Documents now support images! You can include images in two ways:

**Option 1: Use a Public URL**

If you already have images hosted online, simply use Markdown format:

```markdown

```

**Important:** Make sure the URL is publicly accessible so your AI can use it.

**Option 2: Upload Images Directly**

## Pro Tips for Success ğŸ’¡

- **Keep Paragraphs Concise:** The AI works best when knowledge is broken into paragraphs of about 500 characters. This helps it find specific answers more easily.
- **Make Links Useful:** You can add links that your AI can share with users. Be sure to include descriptive text so the user (and the AI) knows where the link goes. For example:
  ```markdown
  [Learn more on our blog](https://your-website.com/blog)
  ```
- **Use Dividers:** Use `---` to create a hard separation between different ideas. This tells your Droid that the following information is a completely new topic.
