## How to Write Sales Scripts

Sales scripts use a simple format in a single text area. The recommended structure is:

```
[situation description]
customer: [customer question]
admin: [your response]
```

**Key points:**

- **Situation (optional):** Briefly describe the scenario to add context
- **Customer:** Write example questions or messages that trigger this script (you can include multiple variations)
- **Admin:** Write the response you want your Droid to follow

**Important:** Your AI uses this as a guide and adapts it naturally â€” it's not a word-for-word script.

## Pro Tips for Success

### 1. Start with Your Main Personality First

![bot personality settings](@/assets/general/setting_bot_personality.png)

Before writing sales scripts, define your AI's main personality in **Advanced Settings** > **Important Info**. Your scripts must align with this foundation.

> **Important:** If your main personality is "Professional Assistant" but your scripts sound like a "Shopping Buddy," your Droid will be inconsistent and confusing!

---

### 2. Cover Common Scenarios

Write scripts for questions you get repeatedly:

- **Off-topic questions:** "Are you a bot?", "Can I speak to a human?"
- **Products you don't have:** "Do you sell X?"
- **Objection handling:** "It's too expensive," "Why choose you?"
- **Brand story:** "Who are you?", "Why should I trust you?"
