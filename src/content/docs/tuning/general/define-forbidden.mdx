## Why Every Business Needs Different Settings

**There are no default forbidden topics** ‚Äî because every business is different.

What's acceptable for one store might be off-limits for another. For example:

- **Some stores don't want the bot to handle price negotiations** ‚Äî they prefer a human salesperson to close the deal
- **Some businesses want the AI to stop at checkout** ‚Äî letting a real person guide the final purchase steps
- **Other stores may restrict refund or complaint discussions** ‚Äî ensuring sensitive issues get personal attention

## Step 1: Define Forbidden Topics

Navigate to **Automation** in the Advanced Settings menu. Here you can configure which topics your Droid should avoid.

![Automation settings showing forbidden topics](@/assets/faq/forbidden.png)

You can list specific:

- **Keywords or phrases** (e.g., "discount", "refund scam", "lawsuit", "price negotiation")
- **Topics you don't want the bot to discuss** (e.g., legal disputes, competitor comparisons, final checkout steps)
- **Triggers** like sensitive words, emojis, or specific user intents

### Real-World Examples

**E-commerce Store:** Add "discount code" or "negotiate price" if you want a human to handle special pricing discussions.

**Hotel Business:** Add "payment" or "checkout" so your staff can personally confirm bookings and process payments.

**Professional Services:** Add "legal advice" or "medical recommendation" to ensure compliance and avoid liability.

> **Remember:** What you restrict depends entirely on your business model and sales strategy. There's no one-size-fits-all approach.

## Best Practices

### Common Forbidden Topics to Consider

Depending on your business, you may want to restrict:

- Legal or compliance-related questions
- Competitor comparisons or negative reviews
- Financial or banking instructions
- Medical or health advice (if not licensed)
- Politically sensitive topics
- Personal data requests

### Keep Your List Updated

As you monitor conversations, you may discover new topics that should be restricted. Regularly review and update your forbidden topics list to improve your Droid's safety and effectiveness.
