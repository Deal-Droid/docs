## Step 5: Grant Organization Access to Your Droid

Now that your organization is set up and populated, it's time to give your team access to your Droid.

Navigate back to your Droid's **Settings** page. You should now see the organizations you're a member of listed under the Organization section.

![Droid organization access settings](@/assets/setting/organization/grant_organization_access.png)

**To grant access:**

1. Check the boxes next to the organizations you want to share with
2. Click **Save** to confirm

That's it! Members of those organizations can now access and manage your Droid.

> **Remember:** Only the Droid owner can add or remove organization access. If you don't see your new organization listed, try clicking **reload** to refresh the list.
