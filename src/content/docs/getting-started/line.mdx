## 4 main steps to connect LINE OA

## Troubleshooting â€” common issues (short)

- Webhook not working: ensure `Use webhook` is enabled, `Webhook URL` is correct (https, no spaces) and saved
- AI not replying but messages reach DealDroid: check `Access Token` is correct/long-lived and inspect DealDroid Chat logs
- Wrong or unexpected replies: check Knowledge Base mapping and sales scripts in DealDroid
- Can't create/copy Channel Secret or Token: check account permissions (admin rights in Provider may be required)
- Duplicate auto-responses: disable LINE Auto-response (In use) before testing

<Aside type="danger">
  If you suspect your Token/Secret are compromised, regenerate the token
  immediately and update DealDroid for security.
</Aside>
