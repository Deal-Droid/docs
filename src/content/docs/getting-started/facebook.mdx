## Step 1: Navigate to Channels and Create Facebook Page

![All Channels page with New Facebook Page button](@/assets/general/facebook/facebook.png)

1. Log in to your DealDroid dashboard
2. Click on **Channels** in the left sidebar menu (highlighted with arrow)
3. You'll see the "All Channels" page showing any channels you've already connected
4. Click the blue **New Facebook Page** button in the top right corner (highlighted with arrow)

<Aside>
You'll also see buttons for other platforms like WhatsApp and LINE OA on this page.
</Aside>

---

## Step 2: Connect with Facebook

After clicking the **New Facebook Page** button:

1. A popup or new window will appear asking you to log in to Facebook
2. You'll be redirected to Facebook's authorization page

<Aside type="tip">
If you already have Facebook channels connected, they'll appear in the list with their channel names. You can connect multiple Facebook Pages to different Droids.
</Aside>

---

## Step 3: Log In to Facebook

When you click to create a Facebook Channel, you'll be redirected to Facebook's login page:

1. **Enter your Facebook credentials** ‚Äî Use the account that has admin access to your Facebook Page
2. Click **Continue** or **Log In**

<Aside type="caution">
Make sure you're logging in with an account that has admin or editor permissions for the Facebook Page you want to connect. If you don't have the right permissions, the integration won't work.
</Aside>

---

## Step 4: Grant Permissions

After logging in, Facebook will ask you to grant DealDroid permissions to access your Pages and send messages:

### Required Permissions:

- üìÑ **Manage and access Page conversations in Messenger** ‚Äî Allows DealDroid to read and respond to messages
- üì¢ **Manage your Pages** ‚Äî Allows DealDroid to access your Page information
- ‚úâÔ∏è **Send messages on behalf of your Page** ‚Äî Allows your AI Agent to reply to customers

Review the permissions carefully, then click **Continue** or **Allow** to grant permissions.

<Aside>
DealDroid only uses these permissions to manage Messenger conversations. Your Page content, posts, and other data remain private.
</Aside>

---

## Step 5: Select Facebook Pages to Connect

![Select Facebook Pages to Connect](@/assets/general/facebook/select_pages.png)

After authentication, **DealDroid will retrieve all Facebook Pages you have access to** ‚Äî including both your personal Pages and Business Pages you manage. At this point, **no connection has been made yet**.

You'll see a screen titled "Select Facebook Pages to Connect" with:

1. A list of all your Facebook Pages (both personal and business accounts)
2. Checkboxes next to each Page
3. Each Page shows its name, category, and profile image

To connect a Page:

1. **Check the box** next to the Facebook Page(s) you want to connect (you can select one or multiple)
2. Click the purple **Connect selected pages** button at the bottom right

<Aside type="tip">
You can select multiple Pages at once. Each selected Page will be added as a separate channel in DealDroid.
</Aside>

<Aside type="caution">
Only after clicking "Connect selected pages" will DealDroid establish the connection with your chosen Pages.
</Aside>

## Testing Your Connection

Want to make sure everything works? Here's how to test:

1. Open your **Facebook Page** on your phone or computer
2. Click **Send Message** to start a Messenger conversation
3. Send a test message like "Hello" or "What products do you have?"
4. Your AI Agent should respond within seconds

If you don't get a response:

- Check that the Facebook Channel is properly connected in DealDroid
- Verify that the correct Droid is assigned to this channel
- Make sure your Droid has Knowledge Base and Products configured
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting the Facebook Page

---

## Managing Multiple Facebook Pages

You can connect as many Facebook Pages as you need:

- **One Page, one Droid** ‚Äî Each Facebook Page should have exactly one AI Agent assigned to it
- **Multiple Pages, multiple Droids** ‚Äî Assign different AI Agents to different Pages (great for multiple brands or locations)

<Aside type="caution">
Each Facebook Page should only be assigned to **one Droid**. Connecting the same Page to multiple Droids will cause customers to receive duplicate messages, creating a poor experience.
</Aside>

To add another Facebook Page:

1. Go back to the **Channels** page
2. Click **New Facebook Page** button again
3. Follow the same steps to authenticate and select another Page

## Troubleshooting

### Can't find my Facebook Page during setup

- ‚úÖ Make sure you have **admin or editor access** to the Page
- ‚úÖ Log in with the **correct Facebook account** that manages the Page
- ‚úÖ If the Page doesn't appear, check your **Facebook Page roles** and permissions

### Want to disconnect a Facebook Page

<Aside type="danger">
Disconnecting a Facebook Page will **permanently delete all conversation history** associated with that channel. This action cannot be undone.
</Aside>

If you still want to proceed:

1. Go to **Channels** in DealDroid
2. Find the Facebook Channel you want to remove
3. Click **Delete** or **Disconnect**
4. Review the warning message
5. Confirm the action

You can also revoke DealDroid's permissions directly from your Facebook Page settings under **Settings > Business Integrations**.

<Aside type="tip">
If you only need to reassign the channel to a different Droid, you don't need to disconnect. Simply change the Droid assignment in the channel settings instead.
</Aside>
