## What Problems Does DealDroid Solve?

Running a business means juggling countless challenges:

- Hiring and training sales staff is expensive and time-consuming
- Staff may leave, forget details, or make costly mistakes
- Customers ask repetitive questions, often after-hours or in different languages
- Slow or poor responses = lost sales
- Scaling human teams to handle growth is unsustainable

> DealDroid was created to solve these problems by giving every business the power to own and train their own AI sales agent ‚Äî one that behaves like your best salesperson, but never sleeps.

## What Does DealDroid Do?

- Detects customer **intent** and sales **funnel stage** (interest, consideration, conversion)
- Recommends **products or services** based on their needs
- Answers **common questions** (pricing, shipping, policies, etc.)
- Handles **objections and hesitations** with smart replies
- Guides customers through **checkout, booking, or inquiry** steps
- Works across platforms like **LINE, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, and website chat**
